About Event
​🌍 Build for Impact: Labor Rights Data Hackathon
Hosted by Data & AI Stockholm x Lovable

​Join us for a collaborative, fast-paced online hackathon where data meets purpose.

​In celebration of May and the importance of labor rights, we’re bringing our communities together for a hands-on session focused on building meaningful projects using real-world datasets. This is not a traditional talk or workshop, it’s a space to explore, experiment, and create something impactful in just a few hours.

​📅 When: Wednesday, 13th of May, 18:00 (CET)
📍 Where: Data & AI Stockholm Discord
⏰ Format: Kick-off + 3-hour hackathon + live community interaction

​What to expect

​We’ll start with a short kick-off session on Discord, where we’ll introduce the theme, walk through the datasets, and share ideas to get you inspired.

​From there, you’ll have 2,5 hours to build, independently. Whether it’s an app, a dashboard, a visualization, or an insight-driven project, the goal is to create something that highlights or explores labor rights through data.

​Throughout the hackathon, we’ll stay connected via Discord, sharing progress, asking questions, and supporting each other along the way.

​What makes this special

​Participants will receive credits from Lovable to support their builds

​A chance to collaborate across two engaged communities

​A focused, high-energy format designed for creativity and execution

​The datasets:

​Employed persons by job tenure

​Employment rate by sex

​Gender pay gap in unadjusted form

​In-work at-risk-of-poverty rate by sex

​Mean weekly hours usually worked per employee by sex -- Annual

​🚨 Please ensure you look at the datasets in advance and are ready to work on them when it's time. You can find the data sets HERE

​Prizes

​🥇 Top 3 projects will be recognized

​🌟 Winner gets the opportunity to present their project in the Lovable Discord community and a small prize from DAIS

​Who is this for?

​This hackathon is designed for people who are comfortable working with data and want to build something meaningful, whether you're a data scientist, engineer, analyst, or have hands-on experience exploring and visualizing datasets.

​Why join?

​Build something meaningful around an important global topic

​Experiment with real-world datasets

​Connect, collaborate, and learn by doing

​Be part of a supportive and creative community
